I’m a designer with a propensity towards stark typography, bold colors, and usability. My passion is designing for screens of any size, with a focus on building smart, simple, and fun experiences. I have a fondness for the work of Bill Moggridge, Dieter Rams, and Karel Martens.

I’ve worked with a number of companies to help ideate, create, build, design, and prototype. I am currently interested in consulting for early stage startups to help define visual language, quickly prototype, and iterate product concepts.
